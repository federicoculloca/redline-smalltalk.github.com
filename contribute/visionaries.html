Visionaries

Towards the tail end of 2012, we launched an IndieGoGo campaign to raise money to help get us to version 1.0. We'd like to thank everyone who has gave some of their hard earned money to Redline Smalltalk. We appreciate the support and faith you showed. From those who gave $10 to those who gave over $1000. We value each and every one of you and hope you enjoy the fruits of our labor and your support. We also appreciate everyone who followed us as a group and as individuals on networks like Twitter and didn't unfollow us despite the repeated calls for support. Hugs to you all.
